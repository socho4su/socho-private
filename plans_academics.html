Steps To Success

  1. Set up a committee dedicated to this cause.
  2. Have monthly sessions when this committee will talk to students to figure out what's annoying them.
  3. Have monthly meetings after every session to figure out what can and can't be done. Set our goals.
  4. Develop a plan to execute the initiative.
  5. Execute it!

Mass Emailing The Entire School

We've worked extremely hard to gain access to all emails this year, and we did just that. We have a list of every single Stuyvesant Student @stuy.edu email address, but we can't use them.
Our IT Department simply did not have the time or resources to develope a platform to handle this volume of emails. We're going to spent the entire summer building a platform, and if need be, we'll purchase resources to assist us in doing so.

So & Chowdhury for SU

The So & Chowdhury will serve to improve student life with respect to education, extra- curricular activities, and other areas of school life. It will work to ensure that the voice of the student body is heard in all discussion of school policy. Under So & Chowdhury, no student who truly shows an interest in investing their time to benefit the Stuyvesant Community will be turned away.
